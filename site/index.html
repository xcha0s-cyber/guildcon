Join us for a day of hacking, learning, and community as we celebrate the first anniversary of Hackers Guild Pittsburgh. Guild Con brings together security professionals, hackers, and enthusiasts for talks, workshops, and our signature Capture The Flag competition.

Venue Accessibility Notice

Important: Our venue is a two-floor facility. Unfortunately, the second floor where all talks are held is currently not wheelchair accessible as there is no elevator available.

Limited streaming: Some talks *may* be streamed locally on our network to the ground floor, depending on individual speaker permissions. However, we cannot guarantee which talks will be available, and no recordings will be made.

We sincerely apologize for these limitations. We understand this severely restricts access to the conference content for wheelchair users and those with mobility challenges. The CTF tournament, networking, and social activities on the ground floor remain fully accessible.

If you have any specific accessibility needs or questions, please contact us and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
