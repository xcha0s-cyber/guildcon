Call for Papers

Share your knowledge at Guild Con 2025

We Want to Hear From You!

Guild Con 2025 is seeking speakers to share their expertise with the Pittsburgh security community. Whether you're a seasoned professional or a first-time speaker, we encourage you to submit your ideas.

Topics We're Looking For:

  • Offensive Security & Red Team Operations
  • Defensive Strategies & Blue Team Tactics
  • Vulnerability Research & Exploitation
  • Reverse Engineering & Malware Analysis
  • Cloud Security & Infrastructure
  • IoT & Hardware Hacking
  • Social Engineering & Physical Security
  • Security Tools & Automation
  • Incident Response & Forensics
  • Privacy & Cryptography

Speaker Guidelines:

  • New Speakers Welcome! Never spoken before? We'll help you prepare
  • Diverse Voices: We especially encourage underrepresented groups
  • Original Content Preferred: Fresh content for our community is ideal
  • Direct Submissions Only: No PR firms - this is about community
  • No Sales Pitches: Keep it technical and educational
  • Make It Accessible: Dive deep but explain your jargon

Talk Formats:

  • Standard Talk: 45 minutes (including Q&A)
  • Lightning Talk: 15 minutes

Important Dates:

  • CFP Opens: September 15, 2025
  • CFP Closes: October 31, 2025
  • Speaker Notifications: November 7, 2025
  • Conference Date: November 22, 2025
