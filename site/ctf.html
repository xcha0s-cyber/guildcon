How It Works

  1. Registration: Sign up on Humanitix (limited to 24 spots)
  2. Team Formation: Players randomly assigned to teams of 3 on the day
  3. Tournament Bracket: Single-elimination format
  4. Rounds:
    • Quarterfinals: 8 teams → 4 teams
    • Semifinals: 4 teams → 2 teams
    • Finals: 2 teams → 1 champion
