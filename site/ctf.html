Capture The Flag Tournament

Test your skills in our single-elimination tournament

Tournament Format

24

Players

8

Teams of 3

Single

Elimination

How It Works:

  1. Registration: Sign up on Eventbrite (limited to 24 spots)
  2. Team Formation: Players randomly assigned to teams of 3 on the day
  3. Tournament Bracket: Single-elimination format
  4. Rounds:
    • Quarterfinals: 8 teams → 4 teams
    • Semifinals: 4 teams → 2 teams
    • Finals: 2 teams → 1 champion

Challenge Categories:*

  • Web Exploitation
  • Binary Exploitation
  • Reverse Engineering
  • Cryptography
  • Forensics
  • OSINT
  • Miscellaneous

*Categories subject to change

Note: The CTF format will not be a traditional CTF. It will have the Hackers Guild PGH touch to it!

Schedule:

  • 9:00 AM - Check-in & Team Assignment
  • 9:30 AM - CTF Briefing
  • 10:00 AM - Quarterfinals
  • 12:00 PM - Lunch Break
  • 1:00 PM - Semifinals
  • 3:00 PM - Finals
  • 5:00 PM - Awards Ceremony
