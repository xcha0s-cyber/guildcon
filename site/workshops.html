Unlocking Bug Bounty – What Every Hacker Should Know

Time: TBA All Levels 10 People Max

Curious about bug bounty programs but not sure where to start? Whether you are brand new to security or already an experienced hacker, this workshop will teach you practical skills and insider tips.

You will learn the key differences between bug bounty hunting, classic penetration testing, and red teaming. We will break down what each approach means and how they fit into the security landscape.

This session will guide you through:

  • How to choose the right target for your bug bounty journey
  • Proven methods for finding real vulnerabilities in applications
  • The step-by-step process for writing and submitting a bug report that gets noticed
  • Essential dos and don'ts of bug bounty hunting, including common mistakes to avoid and expert tips that top hackers use to succeed
  • Maybe we'll do some AI stuff because it's 2025

We'll take a hands-on approach, guiding you through the process of finding your own bugs. With a bit of luck, you might even discover a real vulnerability and have something to report by the end of the workshop.

No matter your experience level, you will leave with a clear understanding of how to get started, how to improve your bug bounty skills, and how to make your first bug report count. Join us to unlock the secrets of bug bounty and take your hacking to the next level.

Price: TBA

Music Workshop - Drum Machine Mastery

Length: TBA Time: TBA 10 People Max

Join us for a new series of workshops that will show you how to get started performing on a drum machine! These hardware instruments are packed with nearly-unlimited capabilities— whether for composing/producing your own music, or playing live for an audience.

What You'll Learn:

  • How to familiarize yourself with drum machine hardware (Akai's MPD218 and MPC2500)
  • Getting started with free first-party Akai software (for PC and Mac)
  • How to make instrumentals and sample sounds or existing songs
  • Custom-made pack of samples to experiment with (provided)

Equipment provided on site! We already have the machines available for hands-on practice. Most programs work with these instruments right out of the box, and we'll ensure compatibility for all attendees.

Get ready for your mastery of drum machines!

Price: $20 (includes Guild Con admission)
